Demi Straulino

Interviewed by Anastasia Kharchenko

  • Did you have pets, if yes then what pet, if not then why?

    The only pet I have ever had was a hermit crab named Hercules.

  • Who did you want to be when you were little?

    • I wanted to be a famous dancer.

  • What is your favorite piece of clothing?

    • My favorite piece of clothing are my shark socks. The mouth goes around my leg and it looks like I'm being eaten by the shark.

  • What is your favorite piece of art?

    • I don't know if I have a favorite piece of art but one of my favorite artists is Moonassi.

  • What is your favorite music genre?

    • My favorite kinds of music are indie, jazz and old blues songs.
