Anastasia Kharchenko

Interviewed by Demi Straulino

  • Anastasia studies at Parsons and is a Communication Design major. She was born in Baku, Azerbaijan (near Armenia and Georgia), but she's spent most of her life in Moscow, Russia.

  • She's taveled to Baku, Moscow, Jim Thorpe (PA, USA), and Washington DC. If she were to have a super power she would want to be immortal, probably to eat her favorite food, pizza for all of eternity and to continue traveling the world.
