Shanjida Kibria

Interviewed by YMuskaan Chaudhry

  • Where did you grow up?

    • Queens, New York

  • What's your favorite part about being an artist?

    • How easy it is to focus while creating art.

  • Which of these weekends sounds ideal?

    • A weekend away with close friends

  • What time do you usually go to sleep

    • 10-11 PM

  • What are the three things you hate about New York City?

    • Rats, roaches, prices

  • And 4 things you love about studying in the city?

    • There's always something to do, large diverse community, tons of food options, lots of opportunity.
