<h2 style="color:dimgrey">Yasmeen AlAyoub</h2>

<h4 style="color:darkgray">Interviewed by Cristina Cepeda Thielmann</h4>

</div>

<div id="left">

<ul>

<li style="color:dimgrey">What is your dream job and why?</p>

<p style="color:darkgray">My dream job is to able to apply different fields of design such as graphic design, fashion, and photography into my work and to communicate with clients on an international scale. </p></li>


<li style="color:dimgrey">Did you always know you wanted to major in a design related field or how did you discover you wanted to? </li>

<p style="color:darkgray">I have always loved design in all of its contexts, whether it be graphic design, fashion, or architecture. I chose Communications Design because I felt like I could explore a broader range of design aspects through this major.</p>


<li style="color:dimgrey">Which is your favorite place in the world and why?</li>

<p style="color:darkgray">My favorite place in the world is my hometown of Kuwait. I grew up there so it's my comfort zone.</p>


<li style="color:dimgrey">4. What's the thing that you love most about New York?</li>

<p style="color:darkgray">The thing I love most about New York is the endless opportunities that exist here. There is something here for everyone and I love that.</p>


<li style="color:dimgrey">Where do you see yourself in 5 years?</li>

<p style="color:darkgray">In 5 years, hopefully I will be getting started on building a name for myself and starting my own design studio in Kuwait.</p>
