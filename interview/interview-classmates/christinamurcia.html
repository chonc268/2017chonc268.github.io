Christina Murcia

Interviewed by Gabriela Zegarra-Ballon

  • What are your favorite shoes to wear on a daily basis?

    My black and white striped Adidas.

  • What is the origin of your name?

    • It was my dad's turn to name a child and it's a common name in Colombia.

  • If you could only see one color for the rest of your life (besides black and white) what would it be?

    • Grey. It's my favorite color and I'd still be able to see most of the school.

  • What is your favorite type of sandwich?

    • One with avocado, toasted challah bread, melted mozarella, arugala, pesto and tomatoes.

  • What would your third genie wish be?

    • To be able to eat as much food as I can without gaining any weight or having any health problems.
